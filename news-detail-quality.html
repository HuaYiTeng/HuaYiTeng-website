ISO9001 Certified Factory with 100% Aging Test Guarantee

Every unit undergoes 100% full-load aging test (min. 2 hours) before shipment. Our ISO9001-certified factory ensures zero-defect delivery, backed by 18 years of manufacturing excellence.

Back to News
Hua Yi Teng's rigorous quality control process includes 100% full-load aging testing (min. 2 hours) for every product

Shenzhen, China — May 28, 2026 — At Hua Yi Teng Technology Co., Ltd., quality is not just a promise — it is a process. The company's ISO9001:2015 certified factory operates under a comprehensive quality management system that ensures every product meets the highest standards before it reaches the customer.

"Zero-defect delivery is the foundation of our reputation," said the company's quality management team. "We are committed to controlling defects within our factory and ensuring they never flow to our customers."

The 100% Aging Test Guarantee

Every power adapter and charger that leaves Hua Yi Teng's factory undergoes a rigorous 100% full-load aging test for a minimum of 2 hours. This process simulates real-world operating conditions, ensuring that each unit performs reliably over its intended lifespan.

The aging test process is fully automated, with aging machines that continuously monitor and record the quality of every product produced. This data-driven approach allows the company to identify and address any anomalies before shipment.

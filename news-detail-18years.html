18 Years of Excellence: Hua Yi Teng Celebrates 2026 Milestone

Founded in 2008, Hua Yi Teng has grown into a leading power adapter manufacturer with ISO9001 certification, a 2,800m² facility, and a monthly production capacity of 900,000 units.

Back to News
Hua Yi Teng's modern 2,800m² manufacturing facility in Shenzhen, China

Shenzhen, China — June 15, 2026 — Hua Yi Teng Technology Co., Ltd., a premier manufacturer of power adapters and chargers, proudly celebrates its 18th anniversary this year. Since its founding in 2008, the company has evolved from a small workshop into a globally recognized power solutions provider, serving clients across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.

A Legacy of Quality and Innovation

Over the past 18 years, Hua Yi Teng has built a reputation for reliability through unwavering commitment to quality. The company operates from a state-of-the-art 2,800m² facility in Shenzhen, China, equipped with advanced automated production lines that ensure consistent, high-precision manufacturing. With over 70 skilled employees and a monthly production capacity of 900,000 units, the company is well-positioned to meet the growing global demand for safe, efficient power solutions.

Did you know? Every power adapter that leaves Hua Yi Teng's factory undergoes 100% full-load aging testing — a rigorous quality assurance process that ensures zero-defect performance and long-term reliability for every single unit.

ISO9001: A Commitment to Quality Management

Hua Yi Teng's ISO9001:2015 certification reflects its dedication to quality management across all operations — from incoming material inspection to finished product testing. The company's quality management system ensures that every product meets international standards before it reaches the customer.

Global Certifications: Market Access Made Simple

To enable seamless market entry for its clients, Hua Yi Teng has obtained a comprehensive suite of international certifications, including:

  • North America: ETL, cETL, FCC, ICES, DOE, CEC, NRCan
  • Europe: CE, GS, CB, UKCA, RoHS, ErP
  • Asia: CCC, PSE, KC
  • Australia: RCM, SAA
  • Quality Management: ISO9001:2015

This "grand slam" of global certifications eliminates regulatory barriers for clients, allowing them to launch products in multiple markets with confidence.

Serving Global Brands

Hua Yi Teng's commitment to quality has earned the trust of leading global brands and Fortune 500 enterprises. The company's core clients include brand owners, wholesalers, and manufacturers across the United States, South Korea, Netherlands, Canada, Italy, Germany, France, and China.

With DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) and DDU (Delivered Duty Unpaid) shipping services, Hua Yi Teng simplifies international logistics by handling the complexities of customs clearance — delivering products directly to clients' warehouses. In addition, we also support domestic delivery or customer self-pickup to accommodate diverse receiving preferences.

Looking Ahead

As Hua Yi Teng enters its 19th year, the company remains committed to its core values: quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With a strong foundation built over 18 years of excellence, Hua Yi Teng is poised to continue delivering reliable power solutions that drive its clients' success.

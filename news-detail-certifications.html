Key Certification Highlights

ETL / cETL (Intertek) — Recognized across the United States and Canada, ETL certification demonstrates compliance with UL 1310 and UL 62368-1 safety standards, ensuring products meet North American electrical safety requirements.

CE-LVD & CE-EMC — The CE mark is mandatory for products sold in the European Economic Area. Hua Yi Teng's compliance covers both Low Voltage Directive (LVD) and Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC) requirements, including EN 55032, EN 55035, and EN IEC 62368-1.

GS (Intertek) — The GS mark (Geprüfte Sicherheit / Tested Safety) is one of the most trusted safety certifications in Germany and the wider European market, particularly valued for LED lighting and outdoor seasonal products.

CB (TÜV) — The CB Scheme is an international system for mutual recognition of test results, reducing the need for repeated testing across different countries. Hua Yi Teng's CB certification is based on IEC 62368-1, with a medical-grade option (IEC 60601-1) also available.

PSE (CQC) — PSE certification is mandatory for electrical products sold in Japan. It ensures compliance with Japanese safety standards, including J62368-1, J55032, and J3000, providing assurance for the Japanese market.

KC — The KC mark (Korea Certification) is mandatory for electronic products sold in South Korea. It demonstrates compliance with Korean safety and EMC requirements, including K 60950-1 and K 62368-1 standards, ensuring products meet the rigorous quality expectations of the Korean market.

CCC (CQC) — China Compulsory Certification (CCC) is required for products entering the Chinese market. Hua Yi Teng's CCC certification covers GB 17625.1, GB 4943.1, and GB/T 9254.1 standards, ensuring full compliance with Chinese regulations.

Why this matters for you: With Hua Yi Teng's certifications already in place, you can bypass the time-consuming and costly process of obtaining individual market approvals. Simply provide your specifications, and we deliver products ready for your target markets.

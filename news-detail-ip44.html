New IP44 Waterproof Adapter for Outdoor Seasonal Lighting

Introducing our latest IP44-rated waterproof adapter, ideal for Christmas lights, inflatable decorations, outdoor displays, and holiday lighting applications. Available in multiple configurations.

Hua Yi Teng's IP44-rated waterproof adapter for outdoor seasonal lighting

Shenzhen, China — June 5, 2026 — Hua Yi Teng Technology Co., Ltd. has expanded its product portfolio with the launch of a new IP44-rated waterproof adapter, specifically designed for outdoor seasonal lighting applications.

The new adapter series is purpose-built for Christmas lights, inflatable decorations, holiday displays, and year-round outdoor lighting installations. With its IP44 splash-proof rating, the adapter provides reliable power in outdoor environments where moisture and weather exposure are common concerns.

Designed for Outdoor Seasonal Applications

The IP44 adapter series addresses the growing demand for safe, durable power supplies for outdoor decorative lighting. Key applications include:

  • Christmas & holiday lights — String lights, net lights, and curtain lights
  • Inflatable decorations — Seasonal yard inflatables and displays
  • Outdoor displays — Storefront and event decorations
  • Yard & garden lighting — Landscape and pathway lighting
  • Festival & event lighting — Temporary outdoor installations

Key Features and Specifications

The IP44 adapter series is available in a wide range of configurations to suit different power requirements:

Power Range 1W – 60W
Input Voltage AC 100-240V
Output Voltage 3V – 30V
Output Current 0.1A – 3A
Ingress Protection IP44 (Splash-proof)
Efficiency Level VI
AC Plug Options US, EU, UK, AU
Warranty 2 Years

Flexible Controller Options

To meet the diverse needs of outdoor lighting applications, the IP44 adapter series offers multiple controller options:

  • Standard adapter — Constant power output for steady lighting
  • 8-function controller — Multiple lighting modes (combination, waves, sequential, slow fade, twinkle, etc.) with timer function
  • IR remote controller — Remote-controlled lighting modes with memory function

Did you know? IP44-rated adapters are specifically designed for outdoor seasonal use, offering protection against splashing water from any direction — making them the ideal choice for Christmas and holiday displays.

Explore the full range of IP44 Waterproof Adapters — available in multiple power ratings and configurations.

Built to Global Standards

All IP44 adapters are manufactured to the highest quality standards and carry a comprehensive range of international certifications, including ETL, cETL, FCC, CE, RoHS, ICES, GS, UKCA, and RCM. This ensures seamless market entry across North America, Europe, and Australia.

The adapters are built with PC fireproof insulation material and are designed for a working life of over 50,000 hours. Each unit undergoes 100% full-load aging testing before shipment, ensuring reliable performance in the field.

OEM/ODM Customization Available

As with all Hua Yi Teng products, the IP44 adapter series is fully customizable. Clients can specify voltage, current, casing design, cable length, connector types, plug configurations, and packaging — all tailored to their brand requirements.

Whether you are a brand owner, wholesaler, or manufacturer, Hua Yi Teng's IP44 adapter series offers a reliable, certified, and flexible power solution for your outdoor seasonal lighting products.

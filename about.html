WHO WE ARE

Hua Yi Teng

Fortune 500 Partner

Founded in 2008, Shenzhen Hua Yi Teng Technology Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive high-tech enterprise integrating product development, production, and sales. We are committed to quality, environment, and customer satisfaction — the cornerstones of our business philosophy.

Our product line includes power adapters and chargers for various electronic devices, serving all-in-one PCs, monitors, smart home devices, LED lighting, audio systems, home appliances, security equipment, inflatables, and festival decorations.

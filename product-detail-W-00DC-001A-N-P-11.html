Available Configurations

Same ultra-compact design, two distinct functional options

Option A: Standard Adapter
For powering small electronic devices directly.
  • Constant voltage output
  • LED indicator: None, Red, or Green (selectable)
  • Output: Customizable voltage & current (3W-6W)
  • DC cable or USB interface
Option B: Smart Battery Charger
For charging 1-cell lithium battery packs.
  • CC/CV charging profile
  • Red → Green indicator (Red: charging, Green: full)
  • Common example: 4.2V/1A (for 1-cell 3.7V Li-ion)
  • Custom voltage & current available within 3W-6W
